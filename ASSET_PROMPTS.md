# Visual Asset Prompts

새 비주얼은 OpenAI 기본 이미지 생성 도구로 제작했다. 모든 이미지는 텍스트와 UI를 포함하지 않는 16:9 게임 배경이며, 노트와 판정 UI는 Canvas/CSS로 렌더링한다.

## Shared Art Direction

- premium children's picture-book gouache illustration
- subtle paper grain, clean shapes, sophisticated editorial finish
- wide landscape with clear central depth for a five-lane rhythm highway
- keep important subjects away from the bottom UI zone
- no text, interface, logo, watermark, pixel art, anime, 3D, or nightclub imagery

## Twinkle

Magical starlight music garden with a deep teal evening sky, an observatory terrace, warm musical lanterns, and a golden melody path rising from the foreground toward the stars. Deep teal, aqua, gold, coral, and natural green.

## Ode to Joy

Joyful sunrise orchestra courtyard with an upright piano, cello, flute, and hand drum placed near the side edges. Warm light connects the instruments like a repeated phrase. Eucalyptus green, sky blue, amber, coral, and cream.

## Moon Waltz

Moonlit forest waltz stage beside a quiet pond with birch trees, warm lanterns, and three repeating arcs of light suggesting triple meter. Forest green, blue-gray, mint, soft rose, and lantern yellow.
